\item {[}ALVL/2018/P1/5{]}

Functions $f$ and $g$ are defined by 
\begin{align*}
f:x\mapsto\frac{x+a}{x+b} & \,\text{for }x\in\mathbb{R},\,x\neq b,\,a\neq-1,\\
g:x\mapsto x & \,\text{for }x\in\mathbb{R}.
\end{align*}

It is given that $ff=g$. 

Find the value of $b$. 

Find $f^{-1}\left(x\right)$ in terms of $x$ and $a$.\hfill{} {[}5{]}
